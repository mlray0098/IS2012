Tableau graphics visualizing various data points about profits and sales of the 50 States

This map shows how each of the states fared regarding profits. California and New York have substantially greater profits than the rest of the states.

This graph shows how New York has fared the past 2 years by its profit. In October 2014, they had the greatest profit.

This graph shows how the profits of New York are divided up within the three categories of product types. Technology is the greatest source of profit for New York.

This graph shows the sales forecast for the year from December 2014 - December 2015 for New York. Most likely due to a lack of data, the forecast is a flat line. The lack of data also leads to a probability in the gaps on the line.
